### assistant
I built an advanced RAG pipeline that stitches together Python, LangChain, Google Gemini 1.5 Flash, FAISS, BM25, and HuggingFace models like all‑MiniLM and BGE‑Reranker, plus UnstructuredIO for PDF parsing. The biggest hurdle was getting clean, semantically coherent text out of multi‑column academic PDFs, so I swapped out basic loaders for Unstructured’s layout‑aware parser and tuned a recursive splitter. I then tackled the semantic versus keyword retrieval gap by creating a hybrid ensemble that pulls the top ten results from both FAISS and BM25, weighting them 70/30. To stop hallucinations, I added a cross‑encoder reranker that trims the noisy chunks down to the top three before feeding them into Gemini with a strict anti‑hallucination prompt. The result is a near‑zero hallucination, highly accurate answer engine that can handle both conceptual and exact‑match queries. This prototype is now a portfolio showcase, ready to be scaled to production with minimal changes.
