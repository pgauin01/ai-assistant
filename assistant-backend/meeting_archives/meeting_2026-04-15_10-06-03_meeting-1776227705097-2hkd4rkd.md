**The Question:** Describe a cloud architecture trade-off you made between performance and cost.

* **Direct Answer:** On the Azure AI project, I chose Azure Functions consumption plan over dedicated App Service to reduce costs while accepting cold start latency.
* **Why Trade-off:** Dedicated hosting would cost ~$70/month for 24/7 availability, but our API had sporadic usage patterns with only 200-300 requests daily.
* **How Implemented:** Used consumption plan ($0 at low volume) + implemented response caching in Redis for frequent queries to mask cold starts.
* **Impact:** Reduced monthly costs from projected $70 to under $5 while keeping P95 latency under 2 seconds, acceptable for our non-real-time use case.
